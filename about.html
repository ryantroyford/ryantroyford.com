ryan troy ford is a designer, illustrator, animator, and screenprinter, with an interest in refined uncleanliness, cartoon complexity, and modern nostalgia.

he enjoys drawing, making music, traveling, and being a human. he promises he'll be better at all of them one day.

currently, he is happily employed, but is always looking for freelance opportunities. some of his favorite things to experiment with are motion graphics, album art, and gig posters.
